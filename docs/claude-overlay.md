# Claude overlay (C11B-lite)

## Role

Claude acts as an **overlay governor** on prepared context: it may emit a typed memo (surface, market, term, caution/downgrade flags). It is **not** a decision engine for this repository.

## This packet

- **Memo-only**: assessments are logged, segmented, and shown in backtest evidence.
- **No proposal rights**: no strikes, widths, structures, or alternatives from Claude.
- **No approval rights**: overlay does not change risk approval, RR, or PMP.
- **No execution rights**: no hooks into execution payloads or MCP transport.

Claude may annotate and recommend stricter posture.  
Claude may not approve, construct, size, or execute a trade.

## Future

A later packet may allow **bounded** structure suggestion only after explicit approval. Until then, treat overlay output as read-only commentary for review.
