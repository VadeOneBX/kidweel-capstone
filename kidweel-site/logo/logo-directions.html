Kidweel — Logo System Directions

Three directions under the 1b Human Authority Layer aesthetic. Review only — nothing here is final. Each reuses established carousel visual grammar (blueprint corner brackets, the human/agent authority-line glyph, the Gate step from the process flow) rather than inventing new iconography. Pick one direction; the full deliverable set (wordmark, square icon, favicon, monochrome, dark-background, LinkedIn-safe crop, GitHub header lockup) will then be finalized as individual asset files for that direction only.

AAuthority Mark

Reuses the exact "human authority" glyph already established in the approved carousel (panels 3 and 6): a circle resting on an arch, crossing a fixed horizontal line. Reads as a checkpoint / reviewer mark, not a face or robot. Directly encodes "human decision layer" and "review before action."

CGate Mark

Draws from the "Gate" step in the process-flow diagram (Context → Candidate → Gate → Review → Decision → Audit). Two posts and a lintel form a minimal gate/checkpoint glyph; one post carries the teal accent — the boundary a candidate must pass through before a human decision is recorded. Encodes "controlled handoff."
